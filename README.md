# 🌍 Aria Alive

### A Personalized Air Quality Health Risk Alert Platform

Aria Alive is a web-based platform designed to provide **personalized air quality risk insights** using real-time PM2.5 data. It helps users understand how air pollution affects their health and offers actionable recommendations to stay safe.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Air pollution, especially **PM2.5 (≤2.5 microns)**, is a major health threat contributing to over **1.67 million deaths annually in India**.

Existing platforms:

* Provide only **general AQI data**
* Lack **personalized health insights**
* Do not offer **real-time actionable guidance**

👉 There is a need for a system that provides **individualized risk assessment** based on personal health and exposure.

---

## 💡 Solution

Aria Alive introduces a **Personalized Air Quality Risk Management Platform** that transforms general air pollution data into **individualized health insights**. Unlike traditional AQI systems, this platform focuses on **user-specific risk assessment** by combining environmental data with personal health factors.

### 🔍 Core Idea

The system bridges the gap between **raw pollution data** and **real-life health impact** by answering:

👉 *“How does today’s air quality affect YOU specifically?”*

---

### ⚙️ How the Solution Works

#### 1. 🧑 User Profiling

Users provide essential inputs:

* Age
* Health conditions (e.g., asthma, cardiovascular issues)
* Time spent outdoors
* Current location

These inputs determine the user’s **individual susceptibility** to pollution.

---

#### 2. 🌍 Real-Time Data Integration

The system integrates:

* **Google AirView+ PM2.5 data**
* **Geospatial APIs (Google Maps)**

This ensures **accurate, real-time, location-based pollution tracking**.

---

#### 3. 🤖 Intelligent Risk Prediction

A **Machine Learning model (Logistic Regression)** analyzes:

* PM2.5 levels
* User health profile
* Exposure duration

👉 It generates a **Personalized Risk Score** representing health impact severity.

---

#### 4. 📊 Actionable Insights

Instead of raw data, users receive:

* 📊 Daily personalized risk levels
* ⏰ Safe time slots for outdoor activities
* 🗺️ Low-pollution route suggestions
* 💡 Health recommendations (mask usage, reduced exposure)

---

#### 5. 🔔 Real-Time Alert System

The system continuously monitors pollution levels and:

* Sends **instant alerts** during high-risk conditions
* Provides **personalized safety guidance**
* Notifies users via **email/SMS**

---

#### 6. 🤖 Chatbot Integration

An optional chatbot feature:

* Answers user queries
* Explains risks in simple terms
* Suggests immediate precautions

---
